Vexon's BOT Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vexon's BOT, operated by Ahmed, collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users interact with the bot and its related services.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of Vexon's BOT on Discord and any related support services.

2. Information We Collect

Based on the current bot code, Vexon's BOT may collect and store information such as:

  • Discord user IDs
  • Discord server IDs
  • Discord channel IDs
  • Discord role IDs
  • XP and points data
  • Daily claim and streak data
  • Birthday entries submitted by users
  • Warning records and moderation-related data
  • Inventory, vault, achievements, trivia wins, steal counts, RPS streaks, and job data
  • Timeout expiry and temporary ban data
  • Message counts
  • Promoted suggestion message IDs
  • Stored IDs for the latest checked YouTube and TikTok uploads

The bot stores much of this information in JSON files, including `config.json` and `data.json`, and supports persistent storage through a data directory such as Railway-mounted storage. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

3. Message Content

Vexon's BOT does not generally store full chat messages for all users as a general archive. However, it does process message content when needed for bot features such as XP tracking, trivia answers, work mini-games, moderation tools, and suggestions. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

In limited cases, user-submitted content may be stored or displayed as part of the bot’s functionality. For example, suggestion text may be reposted when promoted, and moderation warning records may include a reason and timestamp. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

4. How We Collect Information

Information is collected when:

  • Users send commands or messages used by bot features
  • Server administrators configure or use the bot
  • Users submit birthdays, suggestions, or other bot inputs
  • Moderation actions are recorded
  • The bot performs automated checks for Twitch, YouTube, and TikTok notifications

5. How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

  • Operate bot features and commands
  • Track XP, points, rewards, streaks, and profiles
  • Support moderation and anti-raid functions
  • Manage reaction roles, birthdays, and suggestion systems
  • Provide social media notifications
  • Improve reliability, performance, and security
  • Respond to support or deletion requests

6. Storage Method

Vexon's BOT currently uses local JSON-based storage rather than a separate database system. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

7. Third-Party Services

Vexon's BOT may rely on third-party services including Discord, Twitch, YouTube, TikTok, and hosting or infrastructure providers used to run the bot. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

These third parties operate under their own terms and privacy policies.

8. Data Sharing

Vexon's BOT does not sell personal information. Data is only shared when necessary to run the bot, comply with law, or use connected third-party services.

9. Data Retention

Data is kept for as long as reasonably needed to operate the bot, maintain server features, support moderation systems, preserve user progress, resolve disputes, or comply with legal obligations.

Some data may remain stored until manually removed, reset, or deleted by the bot owner.
